

### Final Diagnosis:

- **Anxiety Disorder/Panic Attacks:** The patient has a documented history of anxiety and is currently experiencing panic-like
 symptoms, including chest pain, palpitations, and shortness of breath. These symptoms suggest a potential worsening of anxiety
  or the development of panic disorder, which can manifest physically and mimic cardiac or respiratory issues.

- **Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD):** Both the cardiologist and pulmonologist noted that GERD could be contributing to 
the patient's chest pain and respiratory symptoms. The condition can cause discomfort that mimics cardiac pain and can lead to 
aspiration, resulting in respiratory issues.

- **Stress-Related Symptoms:** The patient's high-stress occupation as an investment banker may be exacerbating both anxiety 
and physical symptoms, including palpitations and shortness of breath. Chronic stress can lead to a range of health issues, 
including cardiovascular and respiratory symptoms, and may require targeted management strategies.
